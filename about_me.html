About me

I have always been interested in innovation, technology and world travel. From a young age I traveled to many countries and throughout my life I have developed interests in geography, architecture, urban planning and innovation in transportation and communication. I ultimately arrived at a crossroads of this wide array of interests in my discovery of Geographic Information Systems and its application to web and mobile technologies.

If you are wondering what heritage my last name belongs to the answer is Spain. My parents are both from Montevideo, Uruguay the capitol city of a small country in South America.
